About

I am a final-year PhD student with VRLab, Beihang University, supervised by Prof. Qinping Zhao. I also work closely with Prof. Bin Zhou and Prof. Jia Li. During my PhD years, I am particularly interested in developing principled optimization algorithms to solve image/video segmentation problems.
